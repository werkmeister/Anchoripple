ABOUT ANCHORIPPLE

HELPING ORGANIZATIONS THAT HELP PEOPLE

Your organization has a story; we want to help you tell it. An organization without a digital strategy is adrift at sea. We use years of industry experience to anchor your non-profit to a digital world.

WHAT WE DO

User Experience

User experience (UX) is the way visitors experience your website. At anchoripple we create engaging experiences that bring your mission face to face with donors who can help

We do this in 3 steps.

1 Learn

We learn everything we possibly can about your mission, your donors, where you've been and where you are going.

2 Create

We create a website that speaks loudly to your cause. An experience you can be proud of.

3 Measure

Finally, we validate the success of what we built through things like stakeholder feedback, donor surveys and usability testing.

Branding

More than just logos and colors, your brand is your identity and gives a voice to your cause. We will help you find your voice. A voice to communicate your mission and the essence of your cause to a digital world.

Design

Design encompasses everything visual about your organization. Your website is your face to the digital world and should echo your organization's culture, personality, and mission. We help you create beautiful digital representations of your organization that attract donors and volunteers to your cause.
